## **Framer UI Design System Summary**

### **Color Palette**

**Primary Colors (Orange Gradient)**
- Primary-1: `rgb(255, 247, 239)` - Very light orange
- Primary-2: `rgb(254, 235, 213)` - Light orange
- Primary-3: `rgb(255, 133, 50)` - Medium orange (This the brand color)
- Primary-4: `rgb(255, 98, 10)` - Strong orange
- Primary-5: `rgb(255, 72, 0)` - Vibrant orange
- Primary-6: `rgb(149, 48, 23)` - Dark orange
- Primary-7: `rgb(120, 42, 22)` - Very dark orange

**Neutral Grays**
- White: `rgb(255, 255, 255)`
- Gray-1: `rgb(245, 244, 246)` - Off-white
- Gray-2: `#e6e6e6` - Light gray
- Gray-3: `#a7a7a7` - Medium gray
- Gray-4: `#8e8e8e` - Darker gray
- Gray-5-7: Various dark grays down to near black

**Accent Colors**
- Purple: 3-level gradient (`#efefff` → `rgb(154, 106, 232)`)
- Blue: 3-level gradient (`#E1F5FE` → `#29B6F6`)
- Yellow, Greenish, Pink, Dawn-pink options available
- Card background: `rgba(13, 13, 13, 0.8)` - Dark semi-transparent

### **Typography**

**Fonts**
- **Headers**: Libre Baskerville (600-700 weights) & Manrope (semibold)
- **Body**: Manrope (regular, medium, semibold)

**Type Scale**
- **Display**: 60px (H1 - 3xl)
- **H1**: 48px (2xl)
- **H2**: 36px (xl)
- **H3**: 24px (lg/md)
- **H4-H5**: 20-18px (sm-md)
- **Body**: 18px, 16px, 14px, 12px (lg → xs variants)

All with line heights: 18-72px and consistent letter-spacing of 0px

### **Spacing & Borders**

**Border Radius**: Not explicitly defined in the system yet (all nodes use default/no borderRadius attribute)

**Spacing**: Consistent use of gaps and padding with various multiples (16px, 24px, 40px increments based on paragraph spacing)

### **Component Library**

Your site has 26+ reusable components including:
- Navigation, Buttons, Badges
- Cards (Work, Testimonials, Pricing, Blog, Member, Case, etc.)
- Footer, Form elements
- Custom visualizations (Radar charts, AI Development sections)
